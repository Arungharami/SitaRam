## Copyright Safety
- All English translations used are pre-1927 publications and confirmed public domain in the United States
- Bangla summaries, moral lessons, and AI guide prompts are original content created by Lead.AI Labs
- No copyrighted modern translations are included

## Religious Sensitivity Guidelines
- App content is purely devotional and educational in nature
- No sectarian claims or inter-religious comparisons are made
- AI Guide always includes disclaimer: "not a religious authority"
- Characters and events are presented with respect aligned with mainstream Hindu tradition
- No depictions of violence, sexual content, or objectionable material
