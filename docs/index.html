About the App

SitaRam is developed by Lead.AI Labs as a donation-supported spiritual and educational tool. Our mission is to make the Valmiki Ramayana accessible to readers worldwide in their native languages.

The AI Research Guide uses Google Gemini for learning and reflection only — it is not a religious authority. All chapter content is sourced from public-domain translations reviewed for accuracy.

Features

  • 7 Kandas — Bala, Ayodhya, Aranya, Kishkindha, Sundara, Yuddha, Uttara
  • English, বাংলা, and Español reading tabs per chapter
  • AI Guide powered by Google Gemini (optional, your API key)
  • Audio narration support (English & Bangla)
  • Research Dashboard — search by character, theme, kanda, moral
  • Personal reflection notes — stored locally on your device
  • Language switcher in Settings
