## 1. Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

SitaRam does not require account creation, login, or registration.

**Data stored locally on your device only:**
- Your Gemini API key (if you choose to enter one) — stored in device SharedPreferences, never transmitted to our servers
- Your personal reflection notes — stored locally, never uploaded
- App preferences (font size, etc.) — stored locally

**AI Guide (Optional):**
If you configure a Gemini API key, your questions are sent directly to Google's Gemini API from your device. SitaRam does not proxy, store, or see these queries. Please review Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect
- No names, email addresses, or contact information
- No location data
- No device identifiers
- No analytics or tracking
- No advertising identifiers
- No payment or financial information (donations link to external services)

## 3. Third-Party Services
- **Google Fonts** — fonts loaded from Google CDN when internet is available
- **Google Gemini API** — only if user provides their own API key
- **Future: Google Play Billing** — if in-app donations are implemented, Google's billing system applies

## 4. Children's Privacy
SitaRam does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app contains no inappropriate content and is suitable for all ages.

## 5. Data Security
All user data is stored locally on the device. No data is transmitted to Lead.AI Labs servers.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
