# SitaRam AI Safety & Scripture Reference Guide

SitaRam integrates an AI Research Assistant to help seekers study moral lessons, character archetypes, and historical contexts of the Valmiki Ramayana. This document outlines the safety guidelines implemented to prevent hallucinations of sacred texts.

---

## 1. Safety Disclaimer

To protect spiritual integrity, the application displays a visible disclaimer on all AI screens:
> **“SitaRam AI Guide is for learning and reflection, not a religious authority.”**

This reminds users that AI responses are generated by language models and should be cross-referenced with authorized translations and scholars.

---

## 2. Prompt Engineering Constraints

When a query is made, `AiService` wraps the user's question with a strict system prompt containing the active chapter context. The system prompt enforces 4 key constraints:

### Rule A: Strict Citation
The AI must reference the specific **Chapter Title** and **Kanda** (e.g. *Bala Kanda - Chapter 1: Valmiki and Narada*) in its answer. It cannot speak generally without grounding the answer in the active chapter.

### Rule B: No Verse Hallucination
The AI is strictly prohibited from inventing verse numbers or claiming exact scriptural translations unless they are present in the provided context text.
- If a user asks: *"What is the Sanskrit text for Rama's virtues in Chapter 1?"*
- If the Sanskrit text is not in the active passage, the AI must reply: *"The exact Sanskrit text for these virtues is not loaded in my active chapter database. However, the chapter summary states that..."*

### Rule C: Respectful Tone
The AI must maintain a respectful, spiritually objective, and calm tone. It should avoid taking dogmatic sectarian stances or answering political questions, keeping discussions focused on character study and moral wisdom (dharma).

---

## 3. Recommended Prompts Integration

To help users begin meaningful research, the app features 7 recommended prompt buttons:
1. **Explain simply** (Overview of chapter plot)
2. **Moral lesson** (The ethical focus or Dharma values)
3. **Explain in Bangla** (Bengali translation and context study)
4. **Character analysis** (Inner motives of characters)
5. **Modern life lesson** (Applying the values to modern life)
6. **Research note** (Historical or theological notes)
7. **Child-friendly explanation** (Simple stories for children)

These buttons automatically generate structured queries, ensuring the AI replies with high-quality formatted insights.
