1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

SitaRam does not require account creation, login, or registration of any kind.

The following data is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted to our servers:

  • Your Gemini API key (if you choose to enter one) — stored in device SharedPreferences, never sent to Lead.AI Labs
  • Your personal reflection notes — stored locally, never uploaded
  • Your app language preference — stored locally

2. AI Guide (Optional)

If you configure a Gemini API key, your research questions are sent directly from your device to Google's Gemini API. SitaRam does not proxy, store, log, or see these queries in any way.

Please review Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No names, email addresses, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No crash reporting sent to our servers
  • No payment or financial information

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Gemini API — only if the user provides their own API key; governed by Google's privacy policy
  • Google Play — distribution platform; governed by Google's policies

5. Children's Privacy

SitaRam does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app contains no inappropriate content and is suitable for all ages.

6. Data Security

All user data is stored locally on the device using Android's SharedPreferences. No data is transmitted to Lead.AI Labs servers at any time.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
