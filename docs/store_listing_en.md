## Full Description (4000 chars max)
**SitaRam** is a free, respectful spiritual companion for the Valmiki Ramayana — one of humanity's oldest and most profound epics. Whether you are a devotee, scholar, student, or curious reader, SitaRam helps you read, listen, and reflect at your own pace.

### What's Inside
- **7 Kandas** — Bala, Ayodhya, Aranya, Kishkindha, Sundara, Yuddha, and Uttara Kanda
- **Multilingual reading** — English, বাংলা (Bangla), and Español (Spanish) side by side
- **AI Research Guide** — powered by Google Gemini for learning and reflection (not religious authority)
- **Audio narration** — English and Bangla audiobook support
- **Research Dashboard** — search by character, theme, kanda, or moral lesson
- **Reflection Notes** — write and save personal spiritual logs locally on your device
- **Settings** — choose your preferred language: English, বাংলা, or Español

### Our Commitments
✔ **Free forever** — no subscription, no paywall, no locked content
✔ **No ads** — ever
✔ **Donation-only** — optional contributions support translation review and hosting
✔ **Public-domain text** — all content sourced from public-domain translations (1891 Griffith edition and equivalents); all OCR reviewed before publication
✔ **AI safety** — the AI Guide is for educational and reflective use only, not a religious authority

### AI Guide — How It Works
SitaRam AI uses Google's Gemini model to answer research questions about the chapter you are currently reading. You can ask it to explain simply, analyze characters, explore moral lessons, compare to modern life, or generate a child-friendly explanation. The AI always shows a disclaimer: *"SitaRam AI Guide is for learning and reflection, not a religious authority."*

Your Gemini API key is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers.

### Privacy
- No account required
- No personal data collected
- Reflection notes stored locally on device only
- Gemini API key stored locally (never sent to our servers)
