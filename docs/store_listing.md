## Full Description (4000 chars max)
SitaRam is a free, ad-free, devotional reading app bringing the complete Valmiki Ramayana to your hands — in both English and Bangla.

Explore all Seven Kandas of the epic: Bala Kanda, Ayodhya Kanda, Aranya Kanda, Kishkindha Kanda, Sundara Kanda, Yuddha Kanda, and Uttara Kanda. Each chapter includes the English translation, Bangla explanation, moral lessons, character insights, and study themes.

**Features:**
• Full Valmiki Ramayana — all 7 Kandas
• English & Bangla bilingual reading
• AI Scripture Guide (powered by Gemini) for learning & reflection
• Research & Study Mode with search by character, theme, or keyword
• Offline-first reading — no internet required for content
• Audiobook player (English narration + Bangla explanation)
• Personal reflection notes saved locally
• Beautiful devotional UI — saffron, gold, and deep maroon
• Zero ads, zero subscriptions, zero paywalls

**AI Guide Disclaimer:**
The SitaRam AI Guide is designed for learning and personal reflection only. It is not a religious authority and does not replace scholarly or traditional religious sources.

**Free & Open:**
SitaRam is completely free. Optional donations support server hosting, translation quality improvements, and audiobook production. All content remains free for everyone, forever.

**Sources:**
English translations are sourced from classical public domain works (pre-1927). All content is reviewed for accuracy and respect.

**For devotees, students, and researchers:**
Whether you are reading the Ramayana for the first time or conducting academic research, SitaRam provides a respectful, structured, and beautiful environment for your journey.

Jai Shri Ram 🙏
